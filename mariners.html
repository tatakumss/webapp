Mariners Polytechnic Colleges

Maritime Education and Beyond

Mariners Polytechnic Colleges is a leading institution in maritime and technical education in the Bicol Region. With a strong foundation in seafaring, engineering, and hospitality programs, it prepares students to be globally competitive professionals.

The college emphasizes discipline, excellence, and international readiness. Mariners provides hands-on training, competent faculty, and industry partnerships to ensure that its graduates are equipped for both local and international opportunities.
