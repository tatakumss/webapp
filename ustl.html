Academic Excellence at UST-Legazpi

University of Santo Tomas-Legazpi (UST-Legazpi) is a Dominican Catholic institution known for its commitment to academic excellence, values-based education, and student-centered learning. It offers a wide range of programs in Arts and Sciences, Business, Education, Health Sciences, Engineering, and Law.

The university provides a dynamic academic environment with state-of-the-art facilities, highly qualified faculty, and innovative curriculum frameworks designed to develop critical thinkers and future leaders. UST-Legazpi emphasizes holistic education, integrating intellectual growth with ethical responsibility and community service.
